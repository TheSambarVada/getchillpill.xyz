Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Subscription

Chill Pill Pro is a monthly subscription at ₹99/month, billed through Razorpay. Your subscription renews automatically on the same date each month.

2. Cancellation

You may cancel your Pro subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your account reverts to the Free tier (30 minutes/month). No partial-month refunds are issued for unused days.

To cancel, contact us via our contact form with your registered email address.

3. Refunds

All subscription fees are non-refundable except in the following circumstances:

Duplicate charge: If you were charged twice for the same billing period, we will refund the duplicate amount within 7 business days.

Service unavailability: If the service is unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours in a billing period due to our fault, you may request a pro-rated refund for that period.

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of the charge via our contact form. Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. Free Tier

The Free tier carries no charge and no refund obligation. Free usage limits (30 minutes/month) are reset on the first of each calendar month.

5. Trial Period

There is no separate trial period. The Free tier serves as an ongoing trial with 30 minutes of transcription per month at no cost.

6. Contact

For refund or cancellation requests, use our contact form. We respond within 2 business days.
