Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Plain English summary: We collect your email to create an account. Audio you record is sent through our server to AI services for transcription and is never stored. We track how many minutes you've used per month for your usage quota. Payment is handled by Razorpay — we don't see your card details.

1. Who We Are

Chill Pill is a voice-to-text transcription app for Windows and macOS. Our website is getchillpill.xyz. For privacy questions, use our contact form.

We do not collect: names, phone numbers, IP addresses, location, device fingerprints, usage analytics, telemetry, or crash reports.

3. How Audio Is Processed

When you activate recording:

  • Audio is captured in device RAM and sent over HTTPS to our proxy server (Railway, hosted in Singapore).
  • The proxy forwards audio to one of the AI services below for transcription. The audio is not stored on our server — it is forwarded in memory and the buffer discarded immediately after the AI response is received.
  • The transcribed text is returned to your device, optionally polished by an LLM, and pasted into your active window. It is then discarded from memory.

Audio is never written to disk on your device or on our server.

4. Keyboard Monitoring

Chill Pill uses a global keyboard listener (via pynput) only to detect your configured hotkey (e.g., F9). We do not log, record, or transmit keystrokes. All key events other than your hotkey are ignored and immediately discarded.

Audio sent to Groq and Sarvam is not retained by default. Groq's Zero Data Retention (ZDR) policy means audio is deleted immediately after transcription.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All API communication uses HTTPS (TLS) in transit.
  • Your auth token is stored locally in %APPDATA%\ChillPillSarvam\settings.json (Windows) or the equivalent on macOS. It is not transmitted to anyone except our proxy server to authenticate your requests.
  • Usage and subscription data is stored in a SQLite database on Railway. Access is restricted to our application.
  • We do not store passwords — Supabase handles authentication and only stores a password hash.

7. Auto-Start & Registry

If you enable "Start Chill Pill on login" in Settings, the app adds a registry entry at HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run\ChillPillSarvam. This is removed when you disable the option or uninstall. No data is collected through this feature.

9. Children's Privacy

Chill Pill is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.
