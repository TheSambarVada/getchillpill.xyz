Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Summary: Chill Pill records audio only when you press the hotkey. Audio is sent to Groq for transcription, then immediately discarded. We do not store, sell, or share your audio or transcribed text. All data stays on your device or is processed ephemerally by Groq.

1. Who We Are

Chill Pill ("we", "our", "us") is a desktop voice dictation application for Windows and macOS, developed and published by Chill Pill. Our website is getchillpill.xyz.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@getchillpill.xyz

2. What Data We Collect

Data Type When Collected Stored Where Retention
Audio recording Only while you hold or toggle the hotkey Temporarily in device memory (RAM) Discarded immediately after transcription
Transcribed text After Groq processes audio Temporarily in device memory (RAM) Discarded after pasting into your active window
Groq API key When you enter it in Settings Locally on your device Until you delete it or uninstall
License key When you activate a license Locally on your device (Windows Registry / macOS plist) Until you deactivate or uninstall
App preferences When you change settings Locally on your device Until you reset or uninstall

We do NOT collect: names, email addresses, IP addresses, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Audio recording — sent to Groq's API (Whisper model) for speech-to-text transcription. The audio is never saved to disk by Chill Pill.
  • Transcribed text — optionally sent to Groq's API (LLaMA model) for text polishing (removing filler words, fixing grammar). The polished text is then typed into your active window and discarded from memory.
  • API key & preferences — stored locally to make the app work. Never transmitted anywhere except to Groq's API for authentication.
  • License key — stored locally. Validated against our license server to confirm purchase. No personal data is sent during validation.

4. Third-Party Services

Chill Pill uses a single third-party service:

Service What We Send Their Retention Their Policy
Groq (GroqCloud API) Audio (for transcription) and text (for polishing) Not retained by default. Audio is deleted immediately after processing. Zero Data Retention (ZDR) available in Groq Console. Groq Privacy Policy

Chill Pill uses your own Groq API key — meaning your data is governed by your direct relationship with Groq under their terms. We are not an intermediary. We do not have access to your Groq account or your API usage.

5. Keyboard & Input Monitoring

Chill Pill uses a global keyboard listener (via the pynput library) solely to detect your configured hotkey (e.g., F9). This is required to start/stop voice recording from any application.

  • We do not log, record, or transmit any keystrokes.
  • The listener only checks whether the pressed key matches your hotkey. All other key events are ignored and discarded.
  • After transcription, Chill Pill uses pyautogui to type the result into your active window. This simulates keyboard input locally and does not transmit any data.

6. Microphone Access

Chill Pill accesses your microphone only when you activate recording by pressing your hotkey. Recording stops immediately when you release or re-press the hotkey.

  • Audio is captured in RAM and sent directly to Groq's API over HTTPS.
  • Audio is never saved to disk on your device.
  • Audio is never stored on our servers — we do not operate any servers that receive your audio.

7. Data Storage & Security

  • All app data (API key, preferences, license) is stored locally on your device in %APPDATA%\ChillPill\ (Windows) or ~/Library/Preferences/ (macOS).
  • API communication with Groq uses HTTPS (TLS encryption) in transit.
  • We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. There is nothing to breach on our end.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

Under GDPR (European Union / EEA)

  • Right to access — request what data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restrict processing — limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object — object to data processing.

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise these rights by simply deleting the app's settings folder or uninstalling the application. No request to us is necessary.

Under CCPA / CPRA (California, USA)

  • We do not sell or share your personal information.
  • We do not use your personal information for targeted advertising.
  • You have the right to know what data is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale — though none of these apply since we don't collect or store personal data on our end.

Under DPDPA 2023 (India)

  • You have the right to obtain information about how your personal data is processed.
  • You have the right to correction and erasure of your personal data.
  • You have the right to nominate another person to exercise your rights.
  • You have the right to file a grievance with us and, if unresolved, with the Data Protection Board of India.

Contact privacy@getchillpill.xyz to exercise any of these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Chill Pill is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used the application, note that no personal data is transmitted to or stored by us.

10. Auto-Start & Registry

If you enable "Start Chill Pill when Windows starts" during installation or in Settings, the app adds a registry entry at:

HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run\ChillPill

This is removed automatically when you uninstall the app or disable the option. No data is collected through this feature.
