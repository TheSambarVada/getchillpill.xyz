Three modes

One hotkey. Three superpowers.

Tap the mode button in the pill to switch. No settings buried in menus.

Transcribe

Exact words, exactly typed

What you say is what gets pasted. Nothing added, nothing removed. Great for notes, quotes, or code comments.

Output
So um I wanted to check if the deploy went through
Transform

Speak casually. Get professional text.

Fillers removed, grammar fixed, tone kept. Speak how you normally talk — Chill Pill handles the cleanup.

You get

Translate

Switch languages mid-thought. Speak naturally in Hindi or Hinglish and get clean English text on the other side.

You get
